Adventure Club

Are you a Bradley University student who is looking for adventure?

Do you enjoy getting out into nature and seeing what it has to offer with a group of like-minded individuals? Then Adventure Club might be the right club for you!

Adventure Club is open to all Bradley students, Contact Us today!

General Information

Most events sponsered by Adventure Club are completely free of charge, and equipment such as tents or hammocks are provided!

Larger or more extensive events - such as a Spring or Fall break trip out of state may require a small fee for gas or food.

No cars necessary! Adventure Club attempts to provide transportation to and from our adventures!
