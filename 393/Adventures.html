Join the Adventures!

Bradley University, has started a club for all of those who love to get out into nature! Join us on nature walks around Peoria, hammocking in the quad, or on one of our several planned adventures during Fall and Spring break!

Adventure Club and Covid 19

Adventure Club is serious about the health of our members, and are taking proper precautions to avoid risks.

Transportation is limitd to 2 people per car, and we ask that all adventurers wear a mask unless it is apporpriate to avoid doing so.
